It is first necessary to confirm whether the DNA structure (Ground) that is the track on which the DNA Walker runs is properly made. In this case, three annealing times, i.e., 1, 5, and 10 hours, were used to find an appropriate annealing condition. Ground A, B, and C were incubated as shown in Figure 1. In order to confirm that Ground A, B, and C were properly bound via C1 and C2 of single-stranded DNA, we carried out the following electrophoresis experiments.
