Experiment E
Material&method

Operation verification of the transpoter

We conducted experiments A to D on each item necessary for the movement of Transporter. Therefore, in Experiment E, we verified whether Transporter is working as designed. Two experiments were conducted with different concentrations of F1 and F2, and the results are shown in Fig.

Figure 1: Results of Experiment E

It can be seen from Figure 1 (A) that when the initial concentrations of F 1 and F 2 are low, the fluorescence intensity does not rise. Therefore, it is understood that the reaction has not advanced to Spot 4. On the other hand, it can be seen from Figure 1 (B) that the fluorescence intensity rises when the initial concentrations of F 1 and F 2 are high. It can be said that the reaction progressed to Spot 4 and Output was released. From the above result, it can be said that the Transporter is operating according to the procedure shown in Design.
