DNA Origami
Figure 1 : Project Image

Figure 1 is our project. This structure was designed with DNA origami. First of all, we designed this structure into two parts, a DNA walker and a plate-shaped origami connected object (Roof) and a DNA walker's footprint (Ground). The design drawing and image are shown below. The design drawing and image of Roof correspond of Figure 2 and Figure 4, and the design drawing and image of Ground correspond to Figure 3 and Figure 5, respectively. We also used caDNAno2 for designing and Cando for creating images and simulation videos.
