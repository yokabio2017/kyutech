Figure 1 shows a summary of our project. This structure was designed with DNA origami. First, we designed this structure to contain two parts: 1) a DNA Walker plus a plate-shaped origami connected object (Roof) and 2) the DNA Walker footprint (Ground). The design drawing and image are shown below (Figures 2 and 4 for the Roof; Figures 3 and 5 for the Ground, respectively). We also used caDNAno2 for designing and Cando for creating images and simulation videos.
