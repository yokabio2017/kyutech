ABSTRACT

我々はDNAナノ構造物が自律的に一方向に駆動する方法を提案する。2本のDNA鎖が部分的に結合し、更に2本鎖部分の両端に1本鎖の領域のあるDNAWalkerと呼ばれる構造体を使用した。Walkerは、ヘアピン構造を持つ同一塩基配列の1本鎖DNA(Spot)が連続的に配置された2本鎖DNA(Ground)の上を走行する。Walkerは自身の1本鎖領域を足がかりとしてSpotと鎖置換反応を起こし隣のSpotへと移動する。すると、再びWalkerとSpotとの鎖置換反応が起こりWalkerは自発的にGround上を進んでいく。Walkerの進み具合は燃料DNAの初期濃度に依存するため、初期濃度を調整することでWalkerの進み具合が調節可能である。我々はDNAWalkerの塩基配列にミスマッチ塩基対を加え、意図しない反応経路を取らないようにした。更に、WalkerをDNAオリガミに接続可能な設計に変更しDNAオリガミ構造物を動かす力として利用できるようにした。複数のWalkerが合わさって構造物を動かす様子をサンタクロースのソリを数匹のトナカイが引っ張る様子に見立て『Nano Santa Claus’s Reindeers』と名付けた。

We proposed a method of DNA nanostructures autonomously driving in one direction. A structure called DNAWalker in which two DNA strands were partially bound and a single-stranded region was present at both ends of the double-stranded portion was used. Walker runs on a double-stranded DNA (Ground) in which single-stranded DNA (Spot) of the same base sequence with a hairpin structure is continuously arranged. Walker uses its single strand region as a toehold and causes a strand displacement reaction with Spot and moves to the next Spot. Then, the strand displacement reaction between Walker and Spot takes place again and the Walker spontaneously travels on Ground. Since the progress of Walker depends on the initial concentration of fuel DNA, the progress of Walker can be controlled by adjusting the initial concentration. We added mismatched base pairs to the nucleotide sequence of DNAWalker so as not to take unintended reaction pathway. In addition, we changed a structure of the Walker in order to connect to the DNA origami so this arrange made it available as a force to move the DNA origami structure. We associated the view of a number of reindeer pulling Santa Claus sled with a plurality of Walkers together moving the structure so we named it "Nano Santa Claus's Reindeers".
